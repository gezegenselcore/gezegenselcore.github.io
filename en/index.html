About

Gezegensel Core is an independent studio brand: AURA focuses on daily wellness, health signals, and AI-guided coaching, while ReFollow brings clearer insights from Instagram data. We turn complex information into simple interfaces and measurable, day-to-day guidance.

Across products we prioritize performance, privacy-minded defaults, and readable data views. AI is used for coaching and summarization—not medical diagnosis or guaranteed predictions.

Gezegensel Core adopts the following principles in every project:

Simplicity: Clear, user-friendly designs free from complexity

Power: High-performance and stable infrastructures

Growth: Continuously updated and evolving systems

Focus: A user-centered approach

We evolve products through user feedback and measurable quality. The Apps section below explains what each product does and where to download it.

In short: Gezegensel Core ships transparent, simple mobile experiences—wellness in daily life and clarity in social data.
